`_install_skill_support_files()` surface instead of inventing new shared
infrastructure, with per-skill `workflows/continue.md` owning full
continuation pipelines including stage-specific fields.

## What was done

Wrote an 862-line self-contained BLUEPRINT-v2.md covering all 7 parts:

- **Part 1:** Per-skill `workflows/continue.md` architecture where each stage

  v2 fix.
